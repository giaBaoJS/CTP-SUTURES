Instructions for Use

Composition:

CARESORB® – Polyglactin 910 Sutures is a multifilament, braided, sterile synthetic absorbable surgical suture composed of a copolymer of 90% glycolide and 10% L-lactide, coated with copolymer of Polyglactin 370 and calcium stearate. The suture is available dyed with FDA-approved color additive D&C Violet No. 2 – CI 60725 or undyed in the natural beige color.

Polyglactin 910 is also named as: Poly(glycolide-co-L-lactide) 90/10, Glacomer 91, Lactomer 9-1, Polyglycolic Acid 9-1, PGLA

Characteristics:

CARESORB® – Polyglactin 910 Sutures provides wound support for 30 days. Complete absorption is accomplished by hydrolysis in 56-70 days.

  • High tensile strength, exceeding USP requirements.
  • AISI 300 series stainless steel (for sutures with attached needles).
  • Ease of handling, smooth passage though tissue.
Indications:

CARESORB® – Polyglactin 910 Sutures are indicated for use in general soft tissue approximation and/or ligation, including use in ophthalmic procedures; but not for use in cardiovascular or neurological tissues.

(A mild inflammatory reaction may occur, which is typical of an endogenous reaction to foreign bodies)

Notice:

Sterilized by Ethylene Oxide (EO). Do not re-sterilize | reuse. Do not use if package is opened or damaged.

Storage:

Recommended storage conditions below 25⁰C, away from moisture and direct heat. Do not use after expiry date. Shelf life: 5 years from date of manufacturing.
