INTRODUCTION
CPT SUTURES CO., LTD.

In business since 1992, CPT is a privately held medical device company in Vietnam, specialising in manufacturing surgical sutures and wound care disposables.

Our mission is to provide safe and effective solutions to surgeries for wound healing, functional recovery and plastic reconstruction; aiming to improve health and quality of life.

With a dedicated workforce, premium raw materials and cutting-edge technology from Germany, we supply products meeting international standards (US-FDA) and featuring superior technical specifications that satisfy customer expectation and legal requirements.

Quality Management System ISO 13485 | FDA 21 CFR Part 820 (QSR)

PRODUCTS

CPT offers a complete range of 600+ suture codes for various surgical procedures: general surgeries, obstetrics & gynecology, orthopedics, cardiovascular, ophthalmic, plastic/cosmetic surgeries…

Our products are trusted by many leading surgeons and healthcare professionals. The quality is comparable to the top brands such as Ethicon (Johnson & Johnson), B.Braun, Covidien (Medtronic).
